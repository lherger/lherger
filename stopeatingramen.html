A healthy recipe book for lazy, broke college students. While I studied abroad in Venice, I created a brand for a publishing company called FreshPress - I want to thank my roommates at the time who helped me with the name - that focused on publishing books created by young artist and writters. The book contains illustrations of recipes meant to not break them bank as well as personal humor and notes from the author. Venice is a city of wall as much as it a city of canals. The people walk the streets; the streets are covered with advertisement. Posters, stuck on a wall.
